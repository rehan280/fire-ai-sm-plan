LinkedIn Strategy

The #1 B2B Lead Engine · Authority Content · High-Conversion DM Funnel

Reality Check
~78% of B2B leads come from LinkedIn. You don't need viral content; you need decision-maker attention.
The 2026 Algorithm Favors: Expertise · Conversations (Comments > Likes) · Early engagement (first 60-90 mins). 2-5 posts/week is ideal.

The 3-Pillar Content System

1. Authority Content

Position FireAI as the ultimate expert in AI + automation. B2B buyers trust knowledge over ads.

  • • "How AI is replacing X job"
  • • "We automated X → saved 40% cost"
  • • Industry tech breakdowns

2. Problem-Aware

Speak to what the audience already feels. The best performing B2B content is problem-driven.

  • • "Your business is losing money because..."
  • • "Manual work is killing your growth"
  • • Pain-point storytelling

3. Proof / Case Studies

Convert attention into leads. B2B buyers want undeniable proof, not just promises.

  • • Before / After results
  • • Client growth statistics
  • • Screenshots of active workflows

Strategic Insights

Winning principles, mistakes to avoid, FireAI category positioning

5 Winning Principles

01

Specificity Survives Algorithm Changes

'How a Mumbai D2C brand found 23% margin leakage in 3 seconds' outperforms 'AI analytics for business' on every platform, every time. Specific beats generic. Always name the vertical, always name the outcome.

02

Consistency Outperforms Quality in 90 Days

A brand posting good content daily for 90 days builds more trust than a brand posting brilliant content twice a month. Show up. The algorithm rewards frequency. The audience rewards reliability.

03

Content Must Solve Before It Sells

Give the full answer in the post, then offer more in the DM. B2B buyers on social media are doing research. Become the answer. The CTA follows naturally when trust is established.

04

The Comment IS the Content

One smart comment on a viral founder's post reaches more relevant eyeballs than a standalone post with zero distribution. The reply game compounds invisibly for 30 days, then explodes in month two.

05

Respect Platform Character

LinkedIn reads like business. Twitter reads like opinions. Instagram looks like a phone. YouTube sounds like a documentary. The same content adapted to each platform's native language outperforms cross-posted content by 3-5x.
